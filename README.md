# Ferns and Petals (FNP) Sales Analysis Dashboard

## Introduction

Ferns and Petals (FNP) is one of India's leading gifting brands, offering flowers, cakes, personalised gifts, plants, chocolates, soft toys, sweets, and other products for occasions such as Birthdays, Anniversaries, Valentine's Day, Raksha Bandhan, Holi, and Diwali. With customers ordering throughout the year, analysing sales performance and customer purchasing behaviour is essential to making better business decisions.

This project presents an **interactive Sales Analysis Dashboard** built entirely in **Microsoft Excel** using **Power Query, Power Pivot, Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, DAX Measures, and Slicers**. The dashboard converts raw transactional data into meaningful business insights, helping stakeholders monitor sales performance, identify seasonal trends, understand customer behaviour, and evaluate product performance.

---

## Problem Statement

The objective of this project is to analyse FNP's sales dataset and build an interactive dashboard to answer key business questions about revenue, customer spending, delivery performance, product sales, and regional performance.

### Business Questions Addressed

- Identify the total revenue generated.
- Calculate the average order-to-delivery time.
- Analyse monthly sales trends.
- Identify the top revenue-generating products.
- Calculate average customer spending.
- Analyse the sales performance of the top 5 products.
- Identify the top 10 cities based on the number of orders.
- Analyse whether higher order quantities impact delivery time.
- Compare revenue across different occasions.
- Identify product popularity for different occasions.

## 📈 Sales Analysis

## 1. Revenue by Occasion

### Insights

- **Anniversary** generated the highest revenue among all occasions.
- **Raksha Bandhan** and **Holi** also contributed significantly to overall sales.
- **Diwali** generated comparatively lower revenue, highlighting an opportunity for stronger festive marketing campaigns.
- Birthday and Valentine's Day maintained steady sales throughout the year.

---

## 2. Revenue by Product Category

### Insights

- ** Colours ** emerged as the highest revenue-generating product category.
- **Soft Toys** and **Sweets** were the second and third highest contributors.
- Categories such as Cakes, Plants, and Mugs generated comparatively lower revenue, indicating potential areas for business improvement.

---

## 3. Revenue by Order Time

### Insights

- Customer purchasing activity remains consistent throughout the day.
- The highest revenue is generated during the **evening hours**, showing that customers prefer placing orders later in the day.
- Revenue dips during late morning hours before increasing again in the afternoon.

---

## 4. Monthly Sales Performance

### Insights

- Sales reached their highest levels during **February** and **August**, reflecting major gifting occasions.
- Revenue remained relatively lower between **April and July**.
- Another noticeable increase was observed during **November**, indicating festive shopping trends.

---

## 5. Top 5 Products by Revenue

### Insights

The five highest revenue-generating products are:

- Magnum Set
- Quia Gift
- Dolores Gift
- Harum Pack
- Deserunt Box

Among them, **Magnum Set** generated the highest revenue, making it the best-performing product.

---

## 6. Top 10 Cities by Orders

### Insights

- **Imphal** recorded the highest number of customer orders.
- **Dhanbad** and **Kavali** also contributed significantly.
- The remaining cities maintained relatively balanced order volumes, indicating a well-distributed customer base.

---

## 7. Customer Spending Analysis

### Insights

The dashboard shows an **average customer spending of ₹3,520.98 per order**, indicating a healthy average purchase value and opportunities for premium product bundles and upselling strategies.

---

## 8. Order Quantity vs Delivery Time

### Insights

The analysis indicates that **higher order quantities do not significantly increase delivery time**. Despite fluctuations in order volume, the average delivery duration remains approximately **5.53 days**, suggesting an efficient and well-managed delivery process.

---

## 💡 Overall Business Insights

- Generated **₹3.52 Million** in revenue from **1,000 orders**.
- Customers spend an average of **₹3,520.98** per order.
- Average delivery time is maintained at **5.53 days**, reflecting efficient logistics.
- Anniversary, Raksha Bandhan, and Holi are the highest revenue-generating occasions.
- ** Colours ** is the top-performing product category.
- Sales show clear seasonal peaks during **February** and **August**.
- Evening hours record the highest customer ordering activity.
- **Magnum Set** is the highest revenue-generating product.
- **Imphal** recorded the highest number of customer orders.
- Delivery performance remains stable even when order quantities increase.

This dashboard provides a comprehensive view of FNP's sales performance, customer behaviour, and product trends, serving as a valuable tool for data-driven decision-making and future business strategy.
